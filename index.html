Guide +

Percentiles

• DataMB Pro percentiles are computed within each player's own league and position, and not within the entire database

Radars

• Search with last name first, it will find 'haaland' but not 'erling'
• Click on a similar player to show his radar

Plots

• Choose metrics and league (if needed)
• Hover on circle to show information
• Click on circle to print player name
• Searched player will be circled in pink
• Click on the toggle to show the median lines
• Once you've selected a league, the search bar will not work for other leagues unless you refresh

Search

• Click on search to update your results

Account Management +

Change your password

• Head to 'Manage Account' in the tool drop-down
• Under password, press "Change"
• Press "Save & Continue"
• Press "Save & Continue" again
• Your password should now have updated

Unsubscribe

• Head to 'Manage Account' in the tool drop-down
• Under DataMB pro, press "Renew, Upgrade or Update Billing"
• Choose "Cancel Subscription"
• Press "Save & Continue"
• Your subscription will be cancelled immediately and you will not be charged for the next billing cycle
• There will be no refunds for current billing cycles, as lined out in the Terms and Conditions
