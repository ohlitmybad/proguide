Percentiles

• Sample 1 : Top 7 European Leagues 🇪🇺
• Sample 2 : All other leagues 🌍

Radars

• Search with last name first, it will find 'haaland' but not 'erling'
• Click on a similar player to show his radar

Plots

• Hover on circle to display information
• Click on circle to print player name
• Click on the toggle to show the median lines

Rank

• Choose a player
• Choose a sample to compare him with
• Click on the blue toggle to go from Per 90 to Total
• Click on the green toggle to go from Ascending to Default

Index

• Choose a metric
• Filter by league and age
• Click on the green toggle to go from Per 90 to Total
